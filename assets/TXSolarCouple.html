About Us

Hey There! We're Kane and Olga Seay, the TX Solar Couple!

Our passion is helping homeowners own their power. We're dedicated to making solar energy affordable for everyone with ZERO up front cost! Our priority is making your solar journey as easy and uncomplicated as possible to fit your busy life!

Solar makes sense

Electricity rates in Texas are rising...and FAST! According to the Dallas Morning News, electricity prices jumped 17% in the past year and are expected to rise as much as 14% in the next year. That means your $150 electric bill is well on its way to being over $200! Going solar protects you from rate hikes, adds value to your home, and helps the planet too. What's not to love? 

Frequently Asked Questions

Please contact us if you cannot find an answer to your question.

The sun will shine on your solar panels and be converted into electricity. That electricity is then sent to an inverter which converts it from direct current to alternating current power. That AC power is then sent to your home and powers your appliances, charges your devices, and more! Then, any remaining power is sent back to the grid and helps power your neighbors' homes!

That's a common misconception! Once upon a time solar was pretty expensive but that's not the case anymore! In most instances, your monthly payments will be less than your current average electric bill. Also, we don't ask for any money down, which makes switching to solar absolutely free! 

We make going solar simple! The first step is sitting down for an appointment with us. We'll take a look at your electric bill and build you a custom system, answer all your questions, and show you how much you'll save. Then, we sign some paperwork and get the process started! We'll perform a site survey to make sure solar power is a good fit for your home. After that, we draw up plans and submit permits to the city, and then comes the most exciting part- install day! After install, we'll get Permission to Operate from your utility company and then your home is officially powered by the sun! If that sounds like a lot, don't worry, we do ALL the work after our initial consultation, you just get to sit back, relax, and finally truly own your power. The entire process typically takes 4-6 weeks from start to finish!
